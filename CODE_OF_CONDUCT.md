# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
experience for everyone who takes part in it, regardless of background or
identity. Contributors, maintainers, and users are all expected to engage with
each other in good faith and with consideration.

## Our standard

`hermes-memory-lancedb-pro` adopts the **Contributor Covenant**, version 2.1, as
its code of conduct. The full text — covering expected behaviour, enforcement
responsibilities, scope, and the enforcement process — is published at its
canonical location:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

By taking part in this project you agree to uphold it.

## Reporting

Concerns about conduct can be raised privately with the project maintainer,
[@iamfoz](https://github.com/iamfoz). All reports will be reviewed and handled
discreetly, fairly, and with respect for the privacy and safety of the reporter.
